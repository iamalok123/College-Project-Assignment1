Biography

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, was born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India. Rising from humble beginnings, he has become one of the most successful actors in Indian cinema history.

With a career spanning over three decades, SRK has appeared in more than 80 films, earning the title "King of Bollywood". His contribution to Indian cinema has earned him numerous accolades, including 14 Filmfare Awards.
